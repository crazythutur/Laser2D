Detailed Description

Reprensent the emitter that will put in the lactice of the 2D_laser.

Reprensent the Emitter that will put in the lactice of the 2D_laser.

Author
Arthur Vallet
Version
1.0
Date
Date
2018/10/26

This object is virtual and can be deffined as a Quantum Dot (DQ) laser or a Quantum Well (QW) laser. Each Emitter is composed by :

  • the pumping_local_rate that represent the portion of the total pump that occur in this Emitter (for exemple by a gaussian distribution).
  • the temperature represent the temperature in the laser
  • the neighboring_lasers regroup all neigbooring laser in direction UP, RIGHT, DOWN and LEFT
