Detailed Description

a Quantum Well (QW) Emitter is an Emitter with QW. It's mean that in each band their is energy levels with a probability of finding an Electron With the Fabrice Philippe Formula (see Electron_presence) it's possible to calculate this probaility knowing the electron number in Conduction Band (CB) thes probability are contained in the electron_presence inititialated by the constructor if they not already exist.
